our users

Pregnancy and childbirth are periods of intense physical and emotional changes, which can cause prolonged feelings of anxiety and stress. Maternal mental health is crucial for the wellness of both the parent and the child, yet it is often unaddressed or completely dismissed.

We saw Capstone as an opportunity to create an experience that promotes emotional well-being for pregnant and postpartum individuals during this challenging time.
